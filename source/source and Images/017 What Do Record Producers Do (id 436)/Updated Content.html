When we refer to other producers, we are talking about people like music agents and record executives. The more connections you make, the more openings you will have. Networking is an essential part of being a successful record producer.

One way to network with other producers is to get involved with jamming sessions at small recording studios. At these sessions, you will meet other record producers looking for an assistant to help them with their projects. If you get to know the right people, they might offer to give you a chance to record an album. This could be your first big break as a record producer. If you impress a record company, you might even get invited to record for them. Eventually, you will be asked to produce their next album.
