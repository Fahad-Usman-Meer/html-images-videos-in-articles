For a singer to be a crowd favourite, they not only need an exceptional voice but also require various other performing skills. From stage confidence, stance to dance, an artist needs to be a complete package. The mix of dance with music is a more than perfect combination.

Singers that can dance are usually people's favourites because of their skills. During live performances and concerts, the element of dance just escalates the entire act. It makes it more delightful to watch and much more entertaining. So, let us take a look at some of the famous singers that have some great moves.

Michael Jackson

I bet you didn't see that one coming. It is more than evident that a list like this would include the world's famous dance icon. Michael Joseph Jackson, "King of Pop," is one of the most famous singers in the world. Known for his infamous moonwalk and iconic dance style, MJ has ruled and still rules the hearts of most of us.

He has been an inspiration for various aspects of life such as fashion, lifestyle, music and dance. As a singer, Jackson started his career in 1971. His success was made evident when he had 13 singles that topped the Billboard Hot 100. He also won 13 Grammys, a Grammy Lifetime Achievement Award, and the Grammy Legend. He also has 39 Guinness World Records and various other honours.

Jackson debuted his most iconic step, the moonwalk, while performing his song "Billie Jean" at the Motown 25: Yesterday, Today, Forever. Jackson has been the idol of various dancers around the world. Jackson achieved so much success not only because of his voice and entertaining lyrics in beats but also the music videos that accompanied the songs.

Jennifer Lopez

You don't need a proof that Jennifer Lopez is a great dancer, she's the judge of the World of Dance, and there can't be any other greater evidence than that of her skills. J.Lo, as she is commonly referred to, is an actress, dancer and singer; she is the complete package of talents. J.Lo started her career in 1991 when she first appeared on "In Living Color" as a fly girl dancer.

She commenced her acting career in 1993 and got her first major role in 1997. Success was not far away from her as she became the first Latin actress to earn over $1 million for a movie. She was soon titled as a Triple Theatre Entertainer because of her skills in the 3 fields of acting, singing and dancing.

She began her music career by releasing her debut album called "On the 6" in 1999 this encouraged the Latin Pop Movement. As she kept on creating music, she started bettering her music videos and eventually performing, showcasing her exceptional dance skills.

Her songs like "Ain't Your Mama" and "On the Floor" showcase her dancing. J.Lo also performs at concerts and live shows which brings out a greater excitement.

Beyoncé

Beyoncé Giselle Knowles-Carter, a woman who is an inspiration for young girls everywhere. She is recognised as an actress, singer, record producer and songwriter. Beyoncé was born and brought up in Houston, Texas. She was a performer since the start; when she was in school, she participated in several dance and singing competitions.

Beyoncé got her stardom when she became the lead vocals of a band called "Destiny's Child" in the 1990s. The band is considered as one among the other best-selling bands. She has also been an inspiration to various new artists. Beyoncé started her solo singing career with her debut album called "Dangerously in Love" in 2013. After the split from Destiny's Child, Beyoncé started creating more music.

She went on to deliver six studio albums and 50 singles which brought her immense success. The music videos assisting the songs included her presenting her dance skills which took the music video to a next level. Her world-famous song "Single Ladies" features her iconic step, and the song "Run the World" showcase her talent.

Shakira

Shakira Isabel Mebarak Ripoll hails from Columbia and is widely known for her singing and dancing. She has also been titled the "Queen of Latin Music" and has been giving us some seriously amazing music to dance to. She released her first English album in 2001, which was named "Laundry Service", which made a sale of over 13 million copies.

We can see her giving great performances in music videos for her top songs like "Hips Don't Lie," "Loca," "Waka Waka," "Chantaje", and "Beautiful Liar." Shakira is considered as one of the top-selling artists as she has sold over 80 million records. In her trophy case, Shakira has 12 Latin Grammy Awards, 7 Billboard Music Awards, 3 Grammy Awards, 6 Guinness World Records and many more accolades.

Shakira has her own star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame. She is an all-rounder artist who is a really great entertainer and performer. She has also worked with the Barefoot Foundation, which

made her eligible for the Latin Recording Academy Person of the Year award in 2011.

Bruno Mars

The man behind "Uptown Funk" had to be on the list. Peter Gene Hernandez, better known as Bruno Mars, is a recognised singer, dancer, songwriter and record producer. He is best known for his elaborate live stage performances. He was born and brought up in Honolulu in Hawaii; he made the move to Los Angeles to attempt a music career in the year 2003.

His hard work paid off, and he got his spotlight in 2010 when B.o.B.'s single "Nothin' on You", which featured Bruno's vocals, blew up. Soon after he released his debut album "Doo-Wops & Hooligans", which got him international attention.

His song "The Lazy Song," "Just the Way You Are," "Locked Out of Heaven," and many more showcase his performing skills. A few of his globally acknowledge songs are "Uptown Funk," "24K Magic," "That's What I Like," "Finesse", and "When I Was Your Man."

Bruno Mars is on the list of top-selling artists as he has sold more than 130 million records. He has eleven Grammy Awards, nine American Music Awards, ten Soul Train Awards, and so many more.

Britney Spears

Britney Jean Spears is famously known for her singing and dancing skills. She was titled as the "Princess of Pop" And signed with Jive Records at the young age of 15 in the year 1997. Her first-ever album, "Baby One More Time", was released in 1999 certified diamond by the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA).

Her second album, "Oops!... I Did It Again," Which was released in 2000, was also a huge success worldwide. Both of these albums are considered as the best selling albums of all time. Her career kept on going uphill from there as she scored 6 albums which topped the Billboard 200. At the same time, she had 4 singers that top the US Billboard Hot 100.

It won't be wrong to say that her heavily choreographed videos are the backbone of her career. She also was awarded the Michael Jackson Video Vanguard Award. Britney Spears also has 13 Guinness World Records, a Grammy Award, 7 Billboard Music Awards, 6 MTV Video boards add also has the iconic star on a Hollywood Walk of fame.

Lady Gaga

Stefani Joanne Angelina Germanotta, or as we all know her Lady Gaga, is a globally acknowledged singer and songwriter. She is famously known for her versatility in music and image reinventions. Gaga was always passionate about music and performed from her teen years at open mics and acted in school plays.

She debuted into the music industry with her album "The Fame", which did get her the fame after her singles "Poker Face" and "Just Dance" topped the charts. She later re-issued this album in an EP which also gave us the infamous "Bad Romance," "Alejandro," and "Telephone."

She also got into acting and played lead roles in movies like "American Horror Story: Hotel"; her performance was appreciated by awarding her the Golden Globe for Best Actress. Her music videos and live performances stood proof of her excellent stage presence and dancing skills.

Lady Gaga has in total sold more than 124 million records and holds several Guinness World Records, and has won eleven Grammy Awards. She also established the Born This Way Foundation as a means to give back to the society.

Justin Timberlake

Justin Randall Timberlake is a well-known musician, songwriter, singer and record producer. Justin was born and brought up in Tennessee and first appeared on television as a child in the shows "The All-New Mickey Mouse Club" and "Star Search." Justin started his music journey when he became the lead vocalist of the band NSYNC.

He debuted his solo album, "Justified", in 2002 and received 2 Grammy Awards for it. It featured one of his most famous songs, "Cry Me a River." He sold over 10 million copies after releasing only 2 albums and then collaborated with several other artists. He then focused on his acting career and starred in movies like "Friends with Benefits," "The Social Network," "In Time" and "Bad Teacher."

He later went on to create a few of his most popular songs like "Mirrors," "Not a Bad Thing," "Can't Stop the Feeling!" and "Filthy." His dancing skills have been showcased in a few of his music videos and on talk shows, "The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon" and "The Late Show With Stephen Colbert." Justin has made sales of over 32 million records and has won several awards like the Emmy Awards, Brit Awards, Grammy Awards and is also the recipient of the Michael Jackson Video Vanguard Award.

Madonna

Madonna Louise Ciccone, lovingly titled as "Queen of Pop", is a songwriter and singer who was a great influence to pop culture. She is best known for her versatile music production, visual presentation and reinvention. She has been an idol for most of the upcoming artists. Madonna was born and brought up in Michigan and decided to move to New York in order to make modern dance a profession in the year 1978.

After making the move, she joined the Breakfast Club, where she played the drums and guitar and occasionally performed vocals. Influenced by music, she released her debut album "Madonna" in 1983. She gained success after she released 5 albums which also won her a Grammy Award. She was also an actress and starred in movies like "League of Their Own" and "Desperately Seeking Susan."

Madonna was titled "The Best-Selling Female Recording Artist" by the Guinness World Records after she made global sales of more than 300 million records. She is also considered as the most successful solo artist to ever be in the history of Billboard Hot 100. She also happens to still remain the "Highest-Grossing Solo Touring Artist of All Time."

Prince

Prince Rogers Nelson was a songwriter, singer, dancer, record producer and multi-instrumentalist. He is widely known for his guitar expertise and fantastic stage presence; he also had a wide range of vocals, which started from the deepest to the highest pitches. Prince was very creative when it came to music and often mixed and match several genres like Hip Hop, Jazz, Rock, Country, Classical, New Wave, Psychedelia, Soul and Pop.

Prince had an astounding output of music as he released a total of thirty-nine albums in his lifetime and yet managed to leave behind some unfinished projects after his death. It is conspired that these projects consist of several albums and more than fifty music videos.

When his career came to a halt, a lively performance at the 2004 Grammy Awards was enough to bring back his mainstream audience. The world lost this gem of an artist to an accidental overdose of fentanyl, and he left a legacy of 150 million records. In his lifetime Prince was awarded the American Music Award for Achievement and the Grammy President's Merit Award. He also has his name in the "Rock and Roll Hall of Fame."
