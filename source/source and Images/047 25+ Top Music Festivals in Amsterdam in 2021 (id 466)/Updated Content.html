The capital of the Netherlands is the world-famous Amsterdam. Amsterdam is seven-hundred and forty-five-year-old city. Amsterdam has had a lot of time to get a lot of things done right and figured out.

Amsterdam is a mature country that has been known for many things. From the red-light district to marijuana, from flowers to bicycles, it has something beautiful, fun, and entertaining for anyone. Amsterdam is an idyllic place to find yourself in and it has a lot to offer in many areas.

Art of Amsterdam

Amsterdam has quite an artistic background and community. Amsterdam is no stranger to the fine arts. Amsterdam has produced some of the best pieces of literature, pottery, painting, writing, architecture, and many other arts. One fine art that Amsterdam is producing more is good music. Music happens to be another one of the seven fine arts.

Music in Amsterdam

The music festivals the Dutch have been putting on are insane. They are at an all-time high and show no signs of slowing down any time soon. The Netherlands also has quite the reputation for being a country that loves to party and have fun. In Europe, the Netherlands is one of the most sought-after places to have fun and to listen to music in. For a long time now, Amsterdam’s music scene has been notoriously off the hook. Amsterdam provides many nightclubs and has some pretty famous ones. Amsterdam is also starting to get known for its raves. These raves are not only in clubs but also in the woods and sometimes abandoned factories and warehouses. There have also been many park parties here.

One thing the general consensus agrees on is Amsterdam is the residence of EDM (Electronic Dance Music) and electronic music. The amount of DJ careers Amsterdam has launched and remained successful is just a testament to how well Amsterdam knows and feels this type of music.

The electronic dance music scene is a big one that has followers all over the world. Since the Dutch produced so many successful EDM DJs, it only made sense that the music festivals had to adapt and evolve with what was happening. There has never been one bad lineup at the more well-known festivals in Amsterdam. These shows, festivals and raves always can be counted on to have a high production value.

Amsterdam has quite a few music festivals every year and the numbers of which are rising. These festivals have brought in some of the biggest electronic dance music artists from all over the world. From the digital festival as well as the awakenings, these venues will not disappoint in the stimulation of the senses.

Due to the Dutch dominance in the DJ world, DJs from Amsterdam and the Netherlands are top tier on the international electronic dance music scene. This makes sure that every year in October, Amsterdam has one of the biggest electronic dance music festivals anywhere. It is called Amsterdam Dance Event. This event brings the whole electronic community to the capital of the Netherlands.

Amsterdam Dance Event

This seems like a good place to begin since the Amsterdam Dance Event is internationally famous. This festival has a duration of one week and numerous parties in October of every year. There are many mini-festivals within this festival to show off all the different kinds of music EDM has to offer. This festival spans across all of Amsterdam.

The DGTL Festival

There is a lot of respect for one of Amsterdam’s biggest brands, DGTL. The respect reaches across Europe and many other parts of the world. On the waterfront of Amsterdam is where this festival is put on. House music, along with techno and electronic dance music fit right in when they are in the NDSM Docklands.

Amsterdam Music Festival

The Amsterdam Music Festival is one of the biggest high points during the week-long party in October during the Amsterdam Dance Event. This festival caters especially to electronic dance music and stadium house music. This festival is in Amsterdam’s Arena. This venue is a large-scale festival venue.

PRJCT Music Festival

RAI Amsterdam is owed a thank-you for putting this party for the world together. This is located just south of the city center. There are usually multiple stages with at least twenty or more artists.

Kingsday Festival

The whole nation celebrates the King’s birthday on the day of April 27. All of the streets are painted bright orange. There have been several performances on this day sure to be remembered.

Awakenings Festival

The Gashouder is in the center of the city of Amsterdam. This venue hosts year-round shows of techno music mostly, but EDM and other kinds as well. The DJs usually end up turning the north-west part of the city into their own dance-floor by the end of the night.

Dance Valley

Since the nineties, Dance Valley place has put on many shows for many people. It continues to showcase some of the most famous artists in all genres and sub-genres of electronic dance music and all other house music.

Don't Let Daddy Know Amsterdam

Don’t Let Daddy Know Amsterdam had its crowing birth in Ibiza. It was conceived from exploring the world. Each host city has a specific audience and DLDK is made for each accordingly. It has been one of the most successful music festivals in Amsterdam.

Amsterdam Live On Stage

This Amsterdam music festival expands the musical spectrum of artists. This is one of the few music festivals that include different genres of music, such as pop and some alternative.

Eighteen Hours Festival

The title says it all. The music festival is one of the longest of its kind. This is one of the most intense parties in the continent of Europe. There are open-air stages that are home to deep house music during the day. At night, techno takes the reins from deep-house when people move indoors.

Loveland Music Festival

Loveland music festival is celebrating its twenty-fifth year. This music festival is quite the showcase of a Dutch party. The beautiful natural scenery is a nice touch to this venue. The lake and wooded area counter the mechanical sound of electronic dance music and techno.

Pacha Music Festival

This is where the Mediterranean meets the Dutch. This music festival has some of the best lights and staging production out of all the festival venues. The Pacha Music festival is an outpost of the world-famous club of Ibiza.

TIK TAK Eclectic Music Festival

This festival is in the open air. It can be found near the ArenA of Amsterdam and other prominent locations, such as the Heineken Music Hall. This venue is known for having super high-energy shows. As its name suggests, TIK TAK Eclectic music festival is made up of an eclectic group of DJs and artists.

Soenda Festival

This music festival has been making memories for over ten years. Soenda festival has been known to feature artists that push the creative boundaries of electronic dance music and the like. This festival aims to please those who are not content with the mainstream forms of music. This venue has one of the most beautiful landscapes as well.

Free Your Mind

This is a music festival that brings you a vast collection of DJs from around the world. Free Your Mind mixes all kinds of techno, deep house music, and electric dance music. This festival is one of the best of its kind. If you go, you will be surround by the Reign River. Free Your Mind is a Dutch pillar in the techno scene.

Into The Woods

This music festival is for those who want to stray away from the boring mainstream. Into the woods has been going on for more than ten years. They create a magical world with new and up and coming electric dance music artists and those under different genres. The showcase many underground DJs and artists as well. Into the woods is one of the best known music festivals in the country.

Mysteryland Music Festival

This festival has a bill that includes only the best DJs and artists around the world. Mysteryland is where it all began in nineteen-ninety-three and has a certain essence to live up to. Mysterland feature underground artists and DJs as well as up and coming artists.

Music On Music Festival

The one and only DJ Marco Carola founded this music festival. This festival is one of the most respected ones in Amsterdam. Music On consist of line-ups that are mostly electronic and spans over a weekend.

Spring Break Amsterdam

This music festival is a multi-venue one. This music festival is divided between eight of Amsterdam’s best music clubs. This festival has hosted over sixty performing artists over the course of its four day span. Many pub-crawls are associated with this festival as well.

Dekmantel Music Festival

This is another music festival that aims to get away from the norms and the mainstream. This music festival is ideal for ravers. It is also ideal for DJs to experiment with new sounds and beats. Dekmantel festival is one of the most respected in Europe.

Milkshake Music Festival

Almost ten years old, Milkshake is a safe haven for anyone and everyone. Every colour, size, shape, age, gender, and sexual orientation come together at this festival and celebrate the different genres and sub-genres of music. This festival has many colours. The main theme in this festival is the freedom of expression. This festival is also disability friendly and hold a disability festival the day before Milkshake Music Festival kick off- this is called Mini Milkshake.

Amsterdam Open Air

This festival is a holy trinity festival. It has music, art, and culinary dishes that serve to work together that aim to promote the Dutch culture. This festival has nine musical areas that celebrate national and world-famous DJs and artists. This gives a good platform for all of the aspiring artists. The Amsterdam Open Air festival has turned a decade just last year.

STRAF_WERK

This is one of the most famous music and dance brands the Dutch have to offer. Straf Werk hosts events all throughout the year. STRAF_WERK has their huge event in the big blowout of summertime. This is one of the more well known festivals in Amsterdam. It gives everyone a perfect excuse to show skin and get in the sun.

Best Kept Secret Festival

This festival is celebrating its eighth year. This music festival is known for gathering some of the best alternative music lineups ever. This festival is put on in the Safaripark Beekse Bergen. This venue includes beaches, green fields, and a lake. The festival spans over three days and has included bands such as The Strokes in the line-up.

The Future of Amsterdam Music Festivals

The future of the music festival scene might seem to be up in the air due to the international pandemic. One thing is for sure though. You can rest assured when our lives are normal again we will be able to party like we used to in Amsterdam and all its clubs and venues.

Amsterdam is the world leader in electronic dance music and other related genres and will continue to be the world leader of these festivals during and after the pandemic.

When the pandemic is over, you can rest assured that Amsterdam and its music festivals will make up for the time we have lost.

Many new and up and coming artists and DJs have been busy creating new beats and sounds in isolation that are waiting to be unleashed in the live events.

All In All

From one October to the next, The Netherlands really know how to have fun and throw a party. Between the Amsterdam Dance Event and all the little festivals sprinkled throughout the rural parts of the Netherlands, The Dutch have really figured something out when it comes to music festivals and making sure all of those that are lucky enough to attend have something to remember for the rest of their lives.
